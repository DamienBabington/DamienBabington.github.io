Ikitsuke:


Available on the App Store for iPhone and iPad.

This app has two overarching components: Places and Groups.

Place Lists:

  • Create custom lists for favorite restaurants, cafes, lounges, and more
  • Make lists either public or private
  • Add places from Google Maps or from other users' lists

Curator Score:

  • Gain recognition as an expert in your field based on how many times users have saved places from your lists

Place Details:

  • View lists in an interactive Map View
  • Write private notes or public reviews for each location
  • Upload your own photos to showcase your experiences
  • Swipe to delete places or lists
  • Open directions in Google Maps with one tap

Groups:

  • Create groups and invite other users
  • Create events
  • Upload photos to an event so you can revisit that specific moment in time
  • Sync events with your calendar
  • Get directions to event locations via Google Maps
  • Easily edit group details or event details

Example Use Cases:

Remote Work

Create a list of your favorite remote work cafes, and take note of each cafe’s noise level, WiFi speed, number of power outlets, etc.

Study Groups

Form a group for skill-sharing or language learning, and schedule study sessions.

Culinary Exploration / Date Night Planning

Discover new restaurants by browsing lists from food enthusiasts you trust. Use the map view if you're looking for places in specific neighborhoods for the perfect date night.

Networking and Collaboration

Establish a group for professionals in your field and organize collaborative work sessions or networking events at your curated list of venues.
